Astar Japan Lab

Astar Japan Lab was established to actively conduct research and development, gather knowledge, and exchange opinions on the development of services and business creation using the Astar Network in Japan, with the aim of further developing the Astar Network.

  • Creating Business Opportunities in Japan with Astar Network

  • Proposed blockchain and NFT use cases

  • Engaging Solution Providers

  • Introduction of overseas VC for web startups from Japan

  • Sharing of Astar use cases and study sessions for participants

  • Connect with the Astar community and ambassadors
